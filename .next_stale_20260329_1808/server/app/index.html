FAQ

Each approved listing passes platform checks and is tied to a verified landlord profile.

Landlords can create accounts immediately, but property listings go live only after admin review.

Can students contact landlords directly?

Yes. Once you identify a property, you can proceed through booking flow and outreach options.

Are there hidden fees?

RentalHub emphasizes transparent rent and charge disclosure before booking decisions.

What if I spot a suspicious listing?

Report it through support, and our admin team will investigate and take action quickly.
